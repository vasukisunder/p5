exquisite corpse

This project was a collaboration between me and two classmates, Aisha and Rudy. I drew the constellation of Orion, Aisha drew a character called Moonflower, and Rudy drew the Corpse Bride. I thought it was interesting that we all drew somewhat dark, moody, and night-themed characters, and I wanted to reflect that in the colors of my sketch.
