lost and found

For this project, I wanted to create a scene that felt like a moment of peace and solitude. I was inspired by the idea of being lost in nature, and how that can sometimes lead to finding yourself. I used a limited color palette to create a sense of calm and tranquility, and I added interactive elements like the moving clouds and the sun that follows the mouse to make it feel more immersive.
