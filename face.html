face generator

My dog Hazel was my inspiration for this project. We experimented with mouse movements this week, and the extreme and bizarre effects it could have on a character's face. I wanted to make sure my sketch still looked dog-like, so I tamped down a lot of the extremity and tried to make sure the dog stayed cute and expressive.
